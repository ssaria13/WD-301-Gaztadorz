Urgent Appeal for Ceasefire to Alleviate Civilian Suffering

The current surge in hostilities involving Israel, Hamas, and other armed factions has inflicted profound harm on civilians. The extensive casualties and widespread destruction in Gaza are unprecedented, causing immeasurable suffering and displacement. In January 2024, the International Court of Justice issued a mandate for Israel to implement six provisional measures aimed at safeguarding Palestinians in Gaza from the threat of genocide. As each day passes, the humanitarian crisis in Gaza deepens, with mounting casualties. We implore all parties involved to agree to an immediate ceasefire to halt the loss of civilian lives and facilitate essential humanitarian aid delivery to Gaza.
